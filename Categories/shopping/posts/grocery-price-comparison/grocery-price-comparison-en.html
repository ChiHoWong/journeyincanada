Introduction

Do you ever wonder which supermarket in Toronto sells grocery products at the cheapest price? We’re going to compare some common products, either with the lowest price or from a common brand, across 6 well-known local supermarkets: No Frills, Freshco, Food Basics, Metro, Loblaws, and Walmart. We will create the same shopping list for each store and calculate the total cost at the end to provide a comparison of supermarket prices.

Our Personal Preference
We used to buy groceries except meat from Nofrills which is next to our home. We buy meat from Metro as it is a lot cheaper than the other supermarket. For the daily necessities, we used to buy them from Walmartbecause it offer more choices than other supermarket. Even though it is not the cheapest, they have flyers every week for certain daily necessities which can reduce the cost and become even more cheaper than other supermarket.

We hope this post can help provide you a brief overview of the supermarket prices!
