Study Plan點寫？

- Word count: <700 words
- 內容：
可參考University of British Columbia 既 Study Plan Guideline：

1) Purpose of Study

  • Why this course?
  • Why Canada?
  • What is your ultimate educational goal? – how can it help you contribute to your home country after study this course

2) Ties to home country
  • Family
  • Property/business

3) Educational & Work Background
  • Educational background
  • Work history
  • How they help with your study in Canada

4) Conclusion

5) Attach Supporting Document
  • Transcripts
  • Graduate certificate of University
  • Official professional certificate of your work

‼️注意‼️
1. 記得千奇唔好話自己係想移民！而係黎讀書，讀完就會返去。
2. 呢份Study plan既format係LETTER, 注意格式同Tone (Formal)

Medical Exam 做upfront既好處和壞處？

好處 — 縮短批核permit時間
唔做upfront既正常流程：
1. 等 ircc 寄 medical exam lette
2. 預約做 medical exam
3. 等 ircc verify
4. process OWP application 		頭3個 steps
有機會1個月先搞掂 medical exam
(因為booking 都比較full)
不過如果大家有充足時間
> 4個月整OWP，唔做 upfront 都完全冇問題

壞處 — 若果OWP被reject, NO REFUND for medical exam

邊到做medical exam?

- 要去指定的 Visa Medical Centre 預約：
  https://www2.ump.com.hk/server.php?id=10&lang_id=2
- 預約排期1 week - 1 month, 旺季e.g Jan- June 會full啲

Proof of Financial Support 要咩文件？

如果父母資助：

如果自資: 只需要個人戶口過往4個月的bank statement和Tuition fee receipt

Step 9: 完成等批

4. 申請需時幾耐？

  • 準備文件：1個月
  • Biometrics：1星期
  • Medical exam: 1個月
  • 審核申請：1-2個月
    (Ps. 通常學生，後生啲都快啲審核完，1個月左右 after biometrics & medical exam 就批)

我地既例子：
5月26日 遞交申請
5月31日 (5日後) 收到biometric letters
6月1日 做Biometrics
(做左Upfront medical exam)
6月26日 批核申請
＝耗時1個月
