Is there a way to save money on every purchase to reduce the cost? The answer is yes. Make sure not to miss out on these 7 cashback and coupon apps when making any purchase.

3. Checkout 51

Introduction

Checkout 51 is a cashback app that offers users rebates on groceries and everyday items. It can only be used in in-store shopping because paper receipts are required to get the cashback.

4. Swagbucks

Introduction

Swagbucks is a popular rewards and cashback app that lets users earn points ("SB") by completing various online activities like surveys, watching videos, shopping online, or searching the web. These points can be redeemed for gift cards or cash via PayPal, providing users with opportunities to earn rewards for everyday online activities.

How to Use it?

5. SPC+

Introduction

SPC+, also known as "Student Price Card Plus," is a membership program primarily designed for students in Canada. It offers discounts and exclusive deals on various products and services, including clothing, food, entertainment, and more. Members can access these savings by showing their SPC+ card or using their digital membership when making purchases at participating retailers or online stores.

How to Use it?

6. Groupon

Introduction

Groupon is a platform offering deals, discounts, and vouchers for various services, activities, and products. Users can find local deals on dining, travel, activities, beauty services, and more.

How to Use it?

1. Sign Up: Create an account on the Groupon website or mobile app.

2. Browse Deals: Explore various deals categorized by location or type—dining, travel, activities, etc.

3. Shop online: Choose a deal you like, read the details, and click "Buy" or "Get Deal." Follow the prompts to complete your purchase.

4. Shop in-store: show the voucher or code at the location

💡Tips: Always review the deal's terms, expiry dates, and redemption instructions before purchasing


7. Reebee

Introduction

Reebee is a digital platform and app that showcases ALL the local store flyers and deals. It allows users to browse current sales, promotions, and discounts from retailers in their area. It is NOT a cashback app but a very convenient app to help you browse all the local deals, compare the price, and plan your shopping trips efficiently.

How to Use it?

Using Reebee is straightforward:
1. Visit reebee website (= flipp.com)/ Download the App

2. Set Location: Allow the app to access your location or manually enter your area to view local flyers.

3. Browse Flyers & View Deals

4. Compare prices and save favourites to plan for your shopping list
